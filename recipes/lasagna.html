Lasagna


Description

A deliciously beautiful dish with the flavor to back up its claim

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

    • Preparation Steps

    1. Make the meat Sauce.
    2. Cook the noodles.
    3. Make the ricotta mixture.
    4. Layer the lasagna.
    5. Cover with foil and bake.
    6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
